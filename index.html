Bio

Hello, I am Nicole Summers. I am currently pursuing my doctorate in Experimental Psychology at Saint Louis University. My concentrations are in Quantitative Methods in Behavioral Sciences and Developmental Psychology. More specifically, my research interests are in parental ethnotheories, mothers' socialization of emotions, community research, and cross-cultural differences between Middle Eastern, Latino/Hispanic, and European American families. My past experience includes, but is not limited to, content analysis, archival data, surveys, interviews, and experimental manipulations.
